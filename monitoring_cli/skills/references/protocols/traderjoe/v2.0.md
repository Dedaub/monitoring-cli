## Cross-chain summary
LB 2.0 on **Avalanche (30 pairs), Arbitrum (43), BNB Chain (10)** — three distinct factory addresses, shared bytecode. Low/no current volume (the AVAX/USDC 20bps pair `0xb5352a39…` was last active in 2023). Discover the handful of pairs via `LBPairCreated` or `getLBPairAtIndex`.

## Proxies
- **Factory/Router/Quoter immutable.** **LBPair instances are standard EIP-1167 minimal proxies** (45-byte clone, prefix `0x363d3d373d3d3d363d73<impl>5af43d82803e903d91602b57fd5bf3`) pointing at the per-chain LBPair impl (Avax `0x49d11cdc…`). This is the **distinguishing on-chain fingerprint vs 2.1/2.2**, whose clones are 97-byte immutable-args clones.

## Detection invariants & gotchas
1. **LB 2.0 events are distinct topic0s from 2.1/2.2** — singular `DepositedToBin`/`WithdrawnFromBin`/`CompositionFee`, and `Swap` `0xc528cda9…` (≠ 2.1/2.2 `0xad7d6f97…`). A 2.1/2.2 monitor will MISS all LB 2.0 activity.
2. **`LBPairCreated` `0x2c8d104b…` is shared with 2.1/2.2** — disambiguate the version by which factory address (`0x6E77932A…`/`0x1886D09C…`/`0x43646A8e…`) emitted it.
3. **`TransferSingle` exists in 2.0 only** (`0xc3d58168…`, = ERC-1155). In 2.1/2.2, single transfers are 1-element `TransferBatch`.
4. Amounts here are **plain `uint256`** (not the packed `bytes32` of 2.1/2.2) — `Swap` carries separate `amountIn`/`amountOut` + a `bool swapForY` direction flag.
