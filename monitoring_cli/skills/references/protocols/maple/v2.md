# Maple 2.0 (`maple-core-v2`) — Topics, Selectors, Addresses (Ethereum + Base)

**Status:** verified 2026-05-29 against the canonical `maple-labs` interface sources (`pool-v2`, `fixed-term-loan`, `open-term-loan`, `fixed-term-loan-manager`, `open-term-loan-manager`, `withdrawal-manager-cyclical`, `withdrawal-manager-queue`, `globals-v2`, `pool-permission-manager`, `syrup-utils`) and the [`maple-labs/address-registry`](https://github.com/maple-labs/address-registry) (`MapleAddressRegistryETH.sol`, `MapleAddressRegistryBASEL2.sol`). **Every topic0 below was recomputed locally with keccak AND confirmed against live `eth_getLogs`**; every selector was confirmed present in deployed bytecode (`eth_getCode`). RPCs: `ethereum-rpc.publicnode.com`, `base-rpc.publicnode.com`.

**Scope:** Maple 2.0 (the Dec-2022 rewrite, internally "V2"; the lending product is now branded **Syrup**) is the **live** Maple protocol. The full lending stack exists on **Ethereum (1)** and **Base (8453)** only. Other chains hold **only bridged tokens** (SYRUP / syrupUSDC / syrupUSDT) — see [syrup-cross-chain.md](syrup-cross-chain.md). For the deprecated 2021 protocol see [v1.md](v1.md).

> **Architecture in one line:** a **Pool** is an ERC-4626 vault; its **PoolManager** (proxy) holds admin + routes funding to one or more **LoanManagers** (FixedTerm + OpenTerm), which fund **Loans** (FixedTermLoan / OpenTermLoan, each a proxy). Redemptions go through a **WithdrawalManager** (Cyclical or Queue). **MapleGlobals** (proxy) is the protocol-wide registry; **PoolPermissionManager** gates lender access. Loan accounting uses an **issuanceRate** model; fees split delegate/platform × management/service.

## 7. Detection invariants & gotchas

1. **Pool is ERC-4626 & non-upgradeable; everything else is a proxy.** Standard `Deposit`/`Withdraw` topics on the Pool; read impl at the 1967 slot on the managers/loans.
2. **FTL vs OTL share event NAMES but not signatures.** `Funded`, `PaymentMade`, `ImpairmentRemoved`, `Repossessed`, `Initialized` all differ between FixedTerm and OpenTerm → **different topic0**. Always disambiguate by (topic0, loan type) — never by name.
3. **`WithdrawalProcessed` has ONE topic0** (`0xddfdc995…`) shared by PoolManager and WithdrawalManagerCyclical. **`SharesRemoved`** (`0x4b171f7f…`) shared by Pool and PoolManager. **`PrincipalOutUpdated(uint128)`** (`0x8e82b759…`) shared by FTLM and OTLM. Disambiguate by emitter.
4. **`UnrealizedLossesUpdated` differs FTLM vs OTLM** — FTLM is `uint256` (`0x58a8faa4…`), OTLM is `uint128` (`0xa6fdea32…`). Different topic0.
5. **Funding/impairment is on the LoanManager, not the PoolManager** (changed across versions). `fund(address)` = `0x23024408`, `impairLoan(address)` = `0x20d61476` on FTLM/OTLM. The V400 PoolManager has neither (verified absent in bytecode).
6. **Two withdrawal models coexist.** Newer pools use **Queue** (request-id based: `RequestCreated`/`RequestProcessed`/`RequestRemoved`); older use **Cyclical** (window based: `WithdrawalUpdated`/`WithdrawalProcessed`). Detect which by the pool's `withdrawalManager` factory.
7. **Deposits often arrive via SyrupRouter**, which emits `DepositData` and forwards to `Pool.deposit` — so the Pool `Deposit` event's `sender` is the router, not the end user. Use `DepositData.owner` for the real depositor, or the SyrupRouter `authorizeAndDeposit` path for permissioned pools.
8. **Lender access is gated by PoolPermissionManager** — `canCall`/bitmaps. A failed deposit may be a permission rejection, not a cap.
9. **issuanceRate accounting:** interest accrues linearly via `issuanceRate` (scaled 1e30 in `PRECISION`); `IssuanceParamsUpdated`/`AccountingStateUpdated` track it. `unrealizedLosses()` reflects impaired/defaulted principal.
10. **Loan funding is OTL "fund in full on accept" vs FTL "fund then drawdown".** OTL `Funded(uint256,uint40,uint40)` (no lender arg); FTL `Funded(address indexed lender, uint256, uint256)`.
11. **Same factory/PPM addresses on Ethereum & Base** for several singletons (PoolPermissionManager, withdrawalManagerQueueFactory, fixedTermLoanFactoryV2, poolDeployerV3) — disambiguate deployments by **chain ID**, not address.
12. **MPL `Transfer`s ≠ V1 activity** — MPL is mid-migration to SYRUP via MplUserActions.
