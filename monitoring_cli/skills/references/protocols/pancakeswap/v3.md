**Status:** topic0/selectors via `cast keccak`/`cast sig` (verified); addresses on-chain re-verified with `cast` vs `publicnode` (2026-05) — V3 factory `feeAmountTickSpacing(2500)=50` and NFPM `symbol()="PCS-V3-POS"` on all 4 chains.
**Scope:** PancakeSwap **V3** (concentrated liquidity — a Uniswap V3 fork). Other versions: [`v2.md`](v2.md), [`stableswap.md`](stableswap.md), [`infinity.md`](infinity.md). Shared CAKE/MasterChef/SmartRouter: [`v2.md`](v2.md) (MasterChefV3 distributes CAKE to V3 LPs).
**Key fact:** V3 is a Uniswap V3 fork, BUT **two pool events are modified** → different topic0:
- **`Swap` adds `protocolFeesToken0/1`** (9 args) → topic0 `0x19b47279…` (≠ Uniswap's `0xc42079f9…`).
- **`SetFeeProtocol` uses `uint32`×4** → topic0 `0xb3159fed…` (≠ Uniswap's uint8 form).
Everything else (Mint/Burn/Collect/Flash/Initialize/PoolCreated/CollectProtocol) is identical to Uniswap V3. Pancake also adds a **0.25% fee tier (2500 → tickSpacing 50)** absent in Uniswap.

## Detection invariants & gotchas
1. **V3 `Swap` topic0 is Pancake-specific (`0x19b47279…`), NOT Uniswap V3's (`0xc42079f9…`).** A monitor watching the Uniswap V3 Swap topic0 will MISS all PancakeSwap V3 swaps. The two extra fields are `protocolFeesToken0`, `protocolFeesToken1`.
2. **`SetFeeProtocol` is `uint32`×4 (`0xb3159fed…`)**, not Uniswap's uint8 form.
3. Mint/Burn/Collect/Flash/Initialize/PoolCreated **are identical to Uniswap/Sushi V3** — disambiguate by the factory/pool address.
4. **0.25% tier**: Pancake enables fee `2500` (tickSpacing 50), which Uniswap V3 does not — a quick fingerprint via `feeAmountTickSpacing(2500)`.
